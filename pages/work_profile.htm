The Cyclist

Simple, fast and low resource consuming Strava alternative app. The Cyclist was designed to be simple and light on battery app which provides all the main features that are provided by Strava. The main focus while developing this app was that the cyclist should be able to record all the details of his journey like average speed, path, distance, time, elevation. The interface is slick and easy to use and even elderly people can use it without any hassle. The cyclist can record his journey and share it with their friends on Whatsapp, Facebook and Instagram.

One Spot Services

OneSpotServices is 7 page application which allows uses to book for a repair service of a number of devices with just a few clicks. This apps is designed to be stylish slick and non obtrusive. The user just has to select the type of device they want a serice for, the date and time-slot they want the service to be and the quantity of devices they want the service for. The app picks up location automatically and let's the user choose the payment method when the user submits the request.
